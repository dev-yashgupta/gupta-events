Our Story

Founded in 1990, Gupta Tent House & Events has been the trusted name in event management for over 35 years. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most respected event management companies in the region.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create unforgettable experiences that bring joy and celebration to people's lives. Today, we continue to uphold that vision with the same passion and dedication that started it all.

With thousands of successful events under our belt, we have built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and attention to detail. From intimate family gatherings to grand corporate celebrations, we handle each event with the same level of care and professionalism.

🎯
