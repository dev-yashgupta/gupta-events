Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book your services?

+

We recommend booking 3-6 months in advance for weddings and major events. For smaller events, 4-6 weeks notice is usually sufficient. However, we do accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability.

Do you provide services outside Delhi?

+

Yes, we provide services across Delhi NCR and nearby cities. Additional travel charges may apply for locations outside our standard service area. Contact us for specific location details.

Can I customize the event packages?

+

Absolutely! We specialize in creating customized packages tailored to your specific needs, preferences, and budget. Every event is unique, and we work closely with you to create the perfect celebration.

What is included in your wedding packages?

+

Our wedding packages typically include venue decoration, tent setup, catering, photography, music and entertainment, and event coordination. Specific inclusions vary by package, and we'll provide detailed information during consultation.

Do you handle destination weddings?

+

Yes, we organize destination weddings across India. We have experience managing events in various cities and can coordinate all logistics, vendor management, and guest arrangements for your destination celebration.
